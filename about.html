What AI Ready Does

When you upload a raw PDF or Word document to an AI assistant — Claude, ChatGPT, or others — the results can be imprecise: wrong page references, missed tables, misread numbers. AI Ready fixes that.

It converts your documents into a structured, auditable format with unique page anchors, integrity fingerprints, and embedded AI reading instructions. Upload the output to any AI assistant and get precise, source-cited answers you can verify.

The Mobile version does all of this in your browser — your documents are never uploaded to any server, because there is no server.

What About Scanned Documents? (OCR)

Some PDFs aren't really text — they're pictures of text: scanned paper, faxes, photographed pages. Reading those requires OCR (Optical Character Recognition) — technology that looks at the image and recognizes each printed character.

The Mobile version does not include OCR — it's heavy processing that needs the AI Ready desktop app (Mac & Windows). What Mobile does instead: it detects scanned pages and tells you exactly which files — and how many pages — need the desktop app, so nothing is silently missed.

⚠️ If you convert a file and see a notice like “14 of 30 pages are scanned images,” those pages need the desktop app's OCR. The readable pages are still captured.

Replacements

Define your own find-and-replace rules, applied automatically to every document and call transcript you process — in addition to the built-in masking. Find any word or phrase and replace it with text you choose; leave the replacement blank to remove it entirely. Examples: “General Motors” → “GM” · “Dr. Johnson” → “Provider”.

Turn on the Replacements switch on the main screen to add your rules — they're saved on your device and apply to all future conversions.

The Gold Master Format

Every document produces a Gold Master file — a complete, structured representation of your original, in three sections:

1 · Document Metadata
Source file name, document type, page count, processing date, and a SHA-256 fingerprint. If the source ever changes, the fingerprint changes — permanently tying this output to a specific version of the original.
2 · Audit Rules
Instructions embedded in the file telling any AI assistant how to read it — what's authoritative, how to cite sources. You don't need to explain anything to your AI.
3 · Source Extraction
The full text, page by page, each page with a unique anchor naming its document. When AI references something, it cites the exact page — not just the document.
📦 Combined Files
Convert multiple files and you also get one unified file with a Source Registry — every input listed with its fingerprint and page count. Ask a question spanning ten documents; the AI knows which file each answer came from.

Document Integrity

🔏 Tamper detection
If the source document is modified after processing, the SHA-256 fingerprint no longer matches. You know exactly what version was processed.
🧾 Audit trail
The combined file's registry lists every input with its fingerprint and page count — a complete chain of custody in one place.

Mobile vs. Desktop

AI Ready is one program with two deliveries. Mobile handles everyday jobs on your phone; the AI Ready desktop app (Mac & Windows) handles the heavy lifting.

✅ Mobile handles
• PDF, Word, PowerPoint & text documents
• Word (DOCX) export of exact PDF text
• Call Transcripts (paste the text)
• Privacy masking (Full / Soft / None)
• Your own find & replace rules
• Combined files with source registry
• Up to 20 files, 25 MB each, per batch
• Works offline once installed
🖥️ Desktop app needed for
• Scanned documents — OCR text recognition
• Audio & call-recording transcription
• Spreadsheets (Excel / CSV)
• Whole folders at once
• Very large files & legal productions
• Large / Custom Project condensing
