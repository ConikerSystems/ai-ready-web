AI Ready Lite

Make your documents AI-ready — right on your iPhone or iPad.

What It Does

AI Ready Lite converts your documents (PDFs and more) into clean, structured Markdown that AI assistants like Claude and ChatGPT can actually read — with page anchors you can cite, document indexes, and verbatim text. Convert a file, save the result, and drop it into your AI chat or knowledge library.

Private by Design

Everything happens on your device, in your browser. Your documents are never uploaded to any server — there is no server. No account, no sign-in, no ads. That makes it safe for sensitive material like legal, financial, and medical documents.

What's Inside

📑 Page-anchored Markdown
Every page gets an anchor so an AI can cite exactly where something came from.
🔍 Document indexes
Parties, dates, amounts, and references mapped to their pages automatically.
🔒 On-device processing
Files never leave your phone or tablet. Airplane-mode private.
⚡ Real conversion engine
The same pipeline as the AI Ready desktop app, running in your browser.

Lite vs. Desktop

The Lite version is built for phone-sized jobs: digital (text-based) documents, a few files at a time. Scanned-document OCR, audio transcription, folder processing, and very large productions are features of the AI Ready desktop app for Mac and Windows.

Get It on Your iPhone or iPad

It's a free web app — no App Store needed:

  1. Open this site in Safari (you're likely here already).
  2. Tap the Share button — the square with an arrow.
  3. Choose “Add to Home Screen,” then tap Add.
  4. Open it from your home screen like any app.
